AuditBoard: A11y + i18n Audit

Accessibility and internationalization scores for the 13 most-active modules in auditboard-frontend, with per-module risks and recommendations.

March 26, 2026

Strongest patterns found
  • assessments and tasks lead the field: both score above 80 on a11y and 85 on i18n, and serve as internal reference implementations.
  • i18n health is consistently stronger than a11y across all 13 modules. The formatMessage + ICU pattern is well established.
  • Luna components provide a solid a11y baseline. Modules that rely heavily on Luna primitives (tasks, compliance) inherit correct ARIA structure with little extra effort.
  • Applanga integration is active: translator context notes and glossaries are already in use where teams have invested in them.
Top cross-cutting gaps
  • Missing aria-live regions for async state changes recurs across admin, hubs, risks, and workspace; no consistent pattern for announcing loading or result counts.
  • Icon-only buttons without aria-label appears in admin, opsaudits, and hubs. @tooltip is not a screen reader substitute.
  • No focus management on modal/drawer open is flagged in workspace and opsaudits; likely present elsewhere as custom overlays grow.
  • dashboard has the lowest scores (a11y 62, i18n 64): status values communicated only by badge color with no text alternative, and API-sourced enum strings with no i18n mapping layer.
  • PROJ#{id} badge pattern is undocumented across admin and tasks; needs a translator exemption note before Applanga flags it.
