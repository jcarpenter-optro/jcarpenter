Redesign the Entity Risk onboarding and conceptual framework
The #1 help cluster— Confusion About Entity Risks (289 records)— and the #2 help cluster— residual risk calculation transparency (254 records)— stem from customers not understanding the library-vs-entity data model. A redesigned guided setup flow with clear tooltips, worked examples, and an in-app "risk hierarchy explainer" would reduce support volume and accelerate adoption.

Address the ROI/adoption gap for existing RiskOversight customers
Limited adoption (386 records) is a leading churn indicator: customers have paid for RiskOversight but are not using it. Deploy a structured "RiskOversight activation program" with CS-led onboarding reviews, automated product usage alerts, and an ERM quick-start template library. The revenue at risk from non-adopting customers (who describe RO as "an expensive data repository") is significant.
